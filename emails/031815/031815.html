WELCOME TO ANGRY EAGLE LODGE & OUTFITTERS

IT’S MORE THAN A FISHING TRIP
IT’S THE ADVENTURE OF A LIFETIME

For more than 30 years, Angry Eagle Lodge & Outfitters has been specializing in remote fly and spin angling adventures in the heart of Southwest Alaska’s Bristol Bay region.*

Ideally situated on the shores of Lake Iliamna, we operate two full-service fishing lodges and cater to anglers of all experience levels. Our large fleet of planes, boats, and ATVs means you’ll always have a great place to cast a line regardless of the weather.

Check out our new website to learn more about Angry Eagle, view interactive video and photo galleries, and plan your ultimate summer vacation.

*Formerly known as Rainbow Bay Resort.
